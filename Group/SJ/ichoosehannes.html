"Who are you?," you ask with little that's left of your voice."
"I am..."

"The Blender-bender."
You shiver as you hear those words spoken aloud.
"I am the one who bends Blender to my will, creating models that defy the laws of physics and imagination."
"And you..."
He turns to you.
"Will be my next masterpiece."
You suddenly understand in what grave danger you are. If Hannes' plan succeeds, he will use you as a model for his twisted creations!
